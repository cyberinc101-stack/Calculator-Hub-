Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Overview

CalcHub provides free calculator tools. All calculations run entirely in your browser — no inputs or results are sent to our servers.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect personal data, financial data, health data, or any inputs you enter into our calculators. No account is required.

3. Analytics

We may use anonymised, aggregated analytics to understand which calculators are most popular. This data cannot identify individual users.

4. Advertising

CalcHub displays third-party advertisements (such as Google AdSense). Ad networks may use cookies for personalised ads. You can opt out at aboutads.info/choices.

5. Cookies

We store your light/dark mode preference in your browser's localStorage. No tracking cookies are set by CalcHub itself.

6. Third-Party Links

CalcHub may link to external resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to us via our website.
