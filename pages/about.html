About CalcHub

What CalcHub Is

CalcHub is a free, independently-built library of calculators and unit converters covering finance, health, math, and everyday measurements. Every tool runs directly in your browser — there's no sign-up, no app to install, and nothing you type is sent anywhere.

Why We Built It

Most calculator sites are cluttered, slow, or bury the actual answer behind pop-ups. CalcHub does the opposite: type a number, get an instant result, with enough context — formulas, reference tables, common conversions — to actually understand what the number means.

How We Keep It Accurate

Every conversion factor and formula on CalcHub is based on standard, published constants. We continually review and expand the calculator library based on what people are actually searching for and using.
