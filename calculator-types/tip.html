How Much Should You Tip?

ServiceSuggested Tip
Restaurant (sit-down)15–20%
Bar / drinks$1–2 per drink, or 15–20%
Food delivery15–20% (min $3–5)
Hair salon / barber15–20%
Taxi / rideshare10–15%
Hotel housekeeping$2–5 per night
Is tip calculated before or after tax?

Most etiquette guides recommend calculating the tip on the pre-tax amount. However, in practice, tipping on the post-tax amount is common and results in a slightly larger tip for the server.
