Frequently Asked Questions

Does a longer vehicle loan term lower my payment?

Yes, stretching a loan from 60 to 72 or 84 months lowers the monthly payment, but increases total interest paid and raises the risk of being 'upside down' on the loan.

Should I include sales tax in the loan amount?

Most lenders roll sales tax, registration, and fees into the financed amount. Add these to your vehicle price before calculating to get an accurate monthly payment.
