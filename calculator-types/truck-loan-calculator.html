Frequently Asked Questions

Are commercial truck loan terms different from a personal vehicle loan?

Commercial truck loans often allow longer terms than personal auto loans, sometimes 6-7 years or more, since trucks used for business have a longer expected service life and higher price tags.

Can a truck loan be used for fleet purchases?

Yes — many lenders offer fleet financing structured similarly to a single-vehicle truck loan, sometimes with volume-based rate discounts for purchasing multiple vehicles at once.

Does a truck loan rate depend on personal or business credit?

It depends on how the loan is structured. Loans taken out by an individual typically rely on personal credit, while loans under a business entity may weigh business credit history, revenue, and time in business alongside or instead of personal credit.
