Frequently Asked Questions

Do VA loans require a down payment?

Most VA loans require $0 down for eligible borrowers, one of the key advantages over conventional mortgages which typically require 3-20% down.

What is the VA funding fee?

The VA funding fee is a one-time charge (typically 1.25%-3.3% of the loan amount depending on down payment and whether it's a first or subsequent use) that helps fund the VA loan program, and can usually be rolled into the loan.
